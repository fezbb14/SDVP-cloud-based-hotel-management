Membership Facilities

Members are entitled to a discount of up to 12% off nightly room rates on all future stays at our lodge by entering their Unique Member ID into the Reservation Code section when making a booking directly through the Petra Flamingo Getaway Lodge website.

Conditions

...
  • Membership is for individual use only and cannot be applied to multiple rooms or bookings over the same dates.
  • Your own Unique Member ID cannot be used by others. The name on the booking must match the name that is attached to the membership.
  • The use of the Unique Member ID may not be available when we are conducting maintenance on our brand website.
