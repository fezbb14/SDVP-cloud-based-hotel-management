Petra Flamingo Getaway Lodge

Positioned over 1100 metres above sea level, Petra Flamingo Getaway Lodge elevates relaxation and luxury to new heights. Petra Flamingo Getaway Lodge is as far from the pressure of everyday life as it is possible to be. Set on 8,000 acres with a backdrop of World Heritage Listed national park, this exclusive Lodge provides the ultimate location for an all-inclusive luxury getaway. Escape to Petra Flamingo Getaway Lodge and enjoy an award winning gourmet experience, relax with a treatment at Spa Anise, explore on a self-guided walk or simply take in the view. Here, relaxation and revitalisation comes naturally.

Our Story

Petra Flamingo Getaway Lodge is Australia’s one of the largest owner-operator of hotel rooms. This gives every guest a guarantee that they are staying in the best accommodation in Australia. Our hotels are award winning and have continually been ranked the best on many traveller review websites. We invest heavily in continually updating our furnishings so our guests will enjoy the latest in design and comfort when staying in our lodge. For over 8-years Petra Flamingo has been the market leader in apartment style hotel accommodation, with convenient location. We are also very excited to announce that Petra Flamingo will be opening up a brand new, luxury hotel in the heart of Melbourne.

Enjoy more space than a typical hotel room, we offer separate bedrooms, lounge and living space for you to enjoy with that special someone, group of friends or the whole family as they comfortably sleep up to eight adults. Our studio and 1 bedroom suites sleep two adults, 2 bedroom suites sleep four adults and the 3 bedroom suites sleep six adults, compared with other hotels who only have small studio rooms. For our hotels that offer both studio and 1 bedroom suite types, we suggest you book a 1 bedroom suite as a little extra cost gives you greater value with a separate bedroom, living and dining space.

Our lodge include a residential sized, spacious gourmet kitchen – perfect to cook your favourite meals whilst you’re travelling. Each of our kitchens has been architecturally designed and include European-style appliances including a ducted range-hood, cooktop, oven, integrated microwave, dishwasher and a full-sized refrigerator. You will find all the essential cooking items and utensils to make use of the residential-style kitchen, including a complete set of glassware, crockery and cookware. All suites have a flat screen HD TV’s in the Living area, as well as TVs in all bedrooms.
